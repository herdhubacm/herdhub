About gestation estimates: Average gestation lengths vary by breed and individual animal. The values used here are breed averages — actual calving can occur 5–10 days before or after the estimated date. Heifers often calve a few days earlier than mature cows. Always watch for signs of labor and have your calving supplies ready at least two weeks before the estimated due date. This tool is for planning purposes only.
