We'll Get The Gate For You

The Problem We Set Out to Solve

The industry hasn't changed.
It's time for a better avenue.

For generations, selling cattle has meant one thing: sort your animals, load a trailer, haul to the sale barn, unload into a pen, and wait for Saturday morning. It's a process that works — but it's built around the auction house's schedule, not yours.

We built Herd Hub to change that. Not to replace the tradition, but to give producers who are ready for something better an avenue that doesn't require sorting, loading, or unloading before a buyer even lays eyes on your animals. Whether a rancher is ready to embrace that or not is entirely their choice — but the option should exist.

The goal is simple: bring the sale barn to your farm, on your timeline — not just the first Saturday of the month.

"A rancher's day never truly ends. Long hours, sleepless nights, the seasons of planning for the next — waiting for rain, or the bottom to dry, the sick pen to clear or the feed truck to arrive."
We built this for those people.
Who We Serve

Built for the 25 to 5,000 Head Operation

🐄
Cow-Calf Producers

Selling calves, pairs, or open females every cycle — list them on your schedule and reach buyers across the country without hauling a single head.

🏆
Seedstock & Showstock

Promoting high-quality bulls, heifers, and show prospects to 4-H families, FFA programs, and breed association members who know what they're looking for.

🌿
Farm to Table

Connecting producers who raise beef direct-to-consumer with the families and communities who want to know exactly where their food comes from.

📢
Industry Advertisers

Sale barns, breed associations, feed companies, and livestock services who want year-round visibility in front of an audience that actually works in the industry.

Our Mission

Technology is the new way of life.
The industry must adapt — or be left behind.

The agriculture industry faces a choice. Embrace the tools that can modernize how livestock is bought, sold, and marketed — or risk being absorbed by large corporations that lack the understanding, the care, and the hands-on knowledge that family ranchers bring to every decision they make.

Farmers and ranchers feed their communities the same way they'd feed their own families. Community by community, that quality can spread — from rolling hills and wide open spaces into every corner of every city — putting profit back into the hands of the people who deserve it most.

Herd Hub exists to provide value, build traffic, promote herds and genetics, and champion the importance of quality, efficient protein production. It has never been more important — or more urgent — to feed communities sustainably and well.

🐂

There Is a Love for This Work

There is a passion that consumes the mind of every serious producer — a drive to do what is right for the animal, what is advantageous to the consumer, and what upholds the standard the industry deserves. It shows up in every early morning, every late-night check on a sick pen, every calculated decision about genetics, feed, and the season ahead.

That dedication is what Herd Hub is built to serve. We are here to give it a platform worthy of the work behind it.
