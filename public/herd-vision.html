About EPD accuracy: EPDs are breed-specific estimates of genetic potential, not guarantees. Accuracy values below 0.50 mean the EPD could shift significantly as more progeny data becomes available. Always consider EPDs alongside visual appraisal, structural soundness, and semen quality. Data sourced from published breed association genetic evaluations. Herd Vision is provided as an educational tool — consult your veterinarian or extension specialist for herd-specific recommendations.
